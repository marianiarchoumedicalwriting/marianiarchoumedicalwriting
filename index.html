About me

I am a medical writer with a PhD in psychiatric genetics and over a decade of experience in academic research. I’ve authored more than 20 first-author peer-reviewed publications and contributed to another 20+ in high-impact journals including Nature Human Behavior, Nature Neuroscience, and Translational Psychiatry. My work has been supported by prestigious funding, including a Sir Henry Wellcome Postdoctoral Fellowship and an R03 from the National Institute on Al...

Over the years, I’ve presented at major international conferences, delivered oral presentations, and written blog posts and science communication pieces aimed at broader audiences. Since 2023, I also serve at an Institutional Review Board (IRB), where I review regulatory compliance for multiple studies each week.

What I’ve always loved most about research is the writing—translating complex findings into clear, meaningful narratives. I’m passionate about delivering accurate, evidence-based content and making science more accessible through thoughtful communication.
